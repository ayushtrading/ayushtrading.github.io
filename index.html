ABOUT US

Delivering Excellence, Cultivating Trust

Welcome to Ayush Trading Company, a trusted name in the processing and trading of agricultural products in India for over 30 years. Specializing in herbs, spices, and garlic products, we have expanded our expertise to include oil seeds, pulses, cereals, and other essential agricultural commodities. Our commitment to quality and excellence has made us a leader in the industry, serving both domestic and international markets.

At Ayush Trading Company, we pride ourselves on our deep-rooted knowledge of the agricultural sector and our ability to deliver products that meet the highest standards. From sourcing to processing, every step of our operation is meticulously managed to ensure that our customers receive only the finest products. Our portfolio includes a wide range of agricultural goods, with a particular focus on herbs, spices, and garlic products, which have become our signature offerings.

We have also pioneered the import and export of these products, bringing the best of Indian agriculture to the global stage. Our international operations are driven by the same principles of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction that have guided us since our inception.

Recognizing the importance of sustainability, Ayush Trading Company forayed into the tyre pyrolysis industry as part of our broader commitment to promoting environmentally responsible practices. By converting waste tyres into valuable by-products, we have contributed to the circular economy and demonstrated our dedication to a sustainable future.

As we continue to grow, Ayush Trading Company remains focused on delivering superior products and services while upholding our values of sustainability and ethical business practices. Whether you are seeking high-quality agricultural products or a reliable partner in the import-export business, Ayush Trading Company is here to meet your needs with professionalism and expertise.
